About Novita

Novita is a comprehensive social welfare web platform designed to help individuals overcome drug addiction, access mental health support, and receive cyber safety assistance in times of need. The name Novita comes from "nova," meaning new, symbolizing new hope, new life, and new beginnings.

Our Core Services
  • Recovery & Mental Health Support - Track progress, connect with mentors and therapists
  • Cyber Safety Protection - Report harassment, abuse, and access digital safety resources
  • Emergency Support System - Immediate access to authorities and crisis counselors

Our Mission

To create a bridge between mental wellness and digital protection, providing a safe, private, and empowering digital space—especially for women and vulnerable individuals. We aim to build comprehensive support systems that address addiction recovery, mental health, and cyber safety in one integrated platform.

Our Vision

We envision a world where no one feels alone in their struggles—online or offline—and where every person has access to hope, help, and healing. By combining recovery support with digital protection, we create a safer, more supportive environment for vulnerable individuals to rebuild their lives.

Why Novita Matters

Safe & Anonymous

Complete privacy protection with anonymous account options for those who need discretion and safety.

Digital Protection

First platform to integrate cyber safety with recovery support, addressing modern digital threats.

Holistic Support

Comprehensive approach addressing addiction, mental health, and digital safety in one platform.

Crisis Response

Immediate access to emergency support during substance relapse, emotional crisis, or cyber threats.
